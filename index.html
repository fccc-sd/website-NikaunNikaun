A Story About Me

It was a luscious spring day when my old school was having a spring dance. As an avid introvert, I wasn't having much fun, and it seemed like the DJ knew it. So he graciousy offered me an oppurtunity to man the smoke machine. I did for a bit before heading back to the dance floor before the fire alarm went off. I didn't realize until much later (Actually, i'm lying, I started to realize on the bus home) that it was probably my fault and vowed to take it to the grave. Years later, I wrote an essay about it and got a pretty good grade. I sometimes wonder if the DJ realized what happend but didn't rat me out. Does he regret it? I wouldn't blame him, to be honest.

About My Cats

I have three cats and I love them with all my heart. Meshki is a black 10 year old cat and as such has been with me through everything. He was shy most of his life but has become the most generally affectionate of them all. He almost never causes trouble and has the cutest little meow. He is the golden child. Gigi is 6 and our only female, she is a diva whose only love is food. As such, her favorite in the family is my dad because he feeds her, though she sometimes cuddles with my sister. Sadly, she is indifferent towards me. She is a highlander. Fettucine is a 1 year old tabby menace with no regard for danger who shares a mutual hatred of Gigi no matter what we try, he sits on my dad's keyboard, goes on the TV to try to touch the celling fan and breaks plates and no matter what, even if we scold him, as long as we touch him he purrs. He also likes being held like he is a human baby being nursed and repeatedly bites random objects, dangerous wires, and food. This then caused him to break one of his teeth. I don't know what's wrong with him but I love him. I love all three of them.

Stuff I've Made

Turns out a giant humanoid poltergeist is trapping them here and not letting them leave for any reason except hunting and gathering. The mansion also has a door under the stairs that seems to transport them to random places frozen in time that give some of the aforementioned kids deja vu, including an empty version of the orphanage our protagonist comes from. How will our cast get out of this? I'm not telling! (This is the aforementioned mostly bad novel that I should rewrite that I mentioned earlier.)
